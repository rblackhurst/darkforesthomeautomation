01 / How it works

What makes DFHA different

LOCAL-FIRST
Nothing leaves your house
Home Assistant runs on a small computer we install in your home. Your devices connect directly to it. No Google, no Amazon, no cloud account required.
YOU OWN IT
Every password is yours
We hand you a printed ownership document with every login. No vendor lock-in. Keep us around for support, or run it yourself.
PRIVACY BY DESIGN
We never watch your cameras
Optional monitoring is uptime-only — we check that your system is healthy, not what's happening inside. Your home stays yours.
